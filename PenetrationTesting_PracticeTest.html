This quiz covers Section 7: Penetration Testing — Pen test purpose & output, team types (Red/Blue/Purple/Physical), knowledge levels (White/Gray/Black Box), Rules of Engagement, Active vs Passive Reconnaissance, the 5-phase attack lifecycle (Initial Access → Pivoting → Lateral Movement → Persistence → Privilege Escalation), key tools, and Security Audits (Internal/External/Third-Party).

Tip: Know Gray Box = most common, Black Box = most realistic, and that the ROE must be signed before any testing begins. Active recon leaves traces; passive recon does not.
